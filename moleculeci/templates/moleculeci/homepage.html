{% blocktrans %}What is Molecule CI?{% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %} Molecule CI proposes to establish a formalism to express the chemical context for which a particular forcefield parameter is applicable (i.e., forcefield usage semantics) and an atom-typing tool that interprets this formalism to generate forcefield parameterizations that are provably correct. {% endblocktrans %}


{% blocktrans %} Key role Molecular simulation plays a key role in understanding the atomistic and molecular level interactions that underlie many natural and man-made materials and processes. {% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %} Easy to use Create your Molecule CI account. Combine it with your molecule repo. Then you are done! {% endblocktrans %}
